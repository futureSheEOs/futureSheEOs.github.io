WHO WE ARE



Tara Bhatia

Co-Founder, Executive Director

Tara is currently a student at Cal, and is studying Computer Science. Through SheEO, she hopes to inspire and educate girls to break glass ceilings from an early age. By combining the intricacies of technology and the fundamentals of entrepreneurship, she is excited to encourage girls to become future “SheEOs”. You can find Tara exploring new restaurants and engrossed in fiction books in her free time.

Almitra Dabholkar

Co-Founder, Executive Director

Almitra is currently a student at UC Berkeley persuing her bachelor's degree in computer science and math. She's interested in the intersection between computer science and entrepreneurship as well as bridging the gender gap in our society. Her passion lies in finding solutions to tough problems, no matter what her role is. In her free time, Almitra loves to play tennis, ski, cook (and eat of course), play her piano, and cheer on the Golden State Warriors!

Mihira Patel

Co-Founder, Executive Director

Mihira is a student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and is thrilled to be working with the SheEO team to host new events in the upcoming year. She has always been passionate about helping bridge the gender gap in the field of technology, and is excited to help solve this problem in entrepreneurship as well. In her free time, she loves exploring new places to eat, spending time with her family, and learning new things!
